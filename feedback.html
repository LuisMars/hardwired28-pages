Playtest feedback

Played a game? Tell us how it went. Everything is optional except one text field, it takes about two minutes, and you don't need an account.

We don't ask for your name, email, or IP. We keep only what you write, to improve the game, and never share it.

Found a technical bug instead? Open an issue on the public issue tracker.
