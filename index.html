The Complex

A vertical megastructure three kilometers tall. Cables hang from every surface. Wired connection is sacred. Wireless is heresy.

The Departments control everything: Resource Allocation, Revenue Operations, Human Capital, Security & Compliance. Below them, subscription debt passes from parent to child. Workers are born owing.

You are not employees. You are contractors: deniable assets operating between factions. You retrieve what was lost and silence what was heard.

Notice: The Complex assumes no liability for injuries, death, or loss of assets during field operations. Contractors without premium coverage assume all medical costs.

Documented Threats

The Complex breeds threats. Some were once workers. Some were never human.

Scrapers Scavengers from the depths where maintenance stopped generations ago

Feral Crews Mutants changed by leaked coolant, green-skinned and free of debt

Wave Callers Wireless cultists who believe the signal is divine

Static Wraiths Corrupted data given physical form, phasing through walls

Feral Automata Obsolete machines still running 600-year-old protocols

Cable Feeders Bioelectric parasites drawn to the warmth of living current

Security Department Notice: Encounters with uncatalogued entities must be reported immediately. Silence is complicity.
